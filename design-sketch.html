ðŸŽ¨ Design Philosophy

  • Warm & Vintage: Rich browns and ambers evoke the classic Les Paul sunburst aesthetic
  • High Contrast: Cream text on dark backgrounds ensures excellent readability
  • Musical Hierarchy: Root notes are caramel (body), chord tones are light amber (center burst), scale notes are medium brown (mid-tones)
  • Natural Gradient: Colors flow from dark edges (#3d1810) through caramel (#8d5524) to bright amber (#f4a460), mimicking the guitar's sunburst
  • Professional Look: Sophisticated earth tones create a premium, instrument-focused aesthetic
  • Consistent Theming: Every element ties back to the guitar's color palette
