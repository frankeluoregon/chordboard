Design Notes

  • Root Notes: Caramel (#8d5524) with cream text (#ffe4b5) and light amber border (#f4a460) - represents the dark guitar body
  • Chord Tones: Light amber (#f4a460) with dark brown text (#3d1810) and caramel border (#8d5524) - represents the bright sunburst center
  • Scale Notes: Medium brown (#5c2f1f) with cream text (#ffe4b5) - represents the mid-tone areas
  • Leading Notes: Amber (#d4722e) with black text (#0a0807) - represents the vibrant orange gradient
  • Background: Deep black (#0a0807) to deep brown (#1a1410) - represents the darkness around the guitar
  • Panels: Dark brown (#2a1f1a) with medium brown borders (#3d2f27) - represents the wood grain
  • Active Elements: Caramel (#8d5524) to amber (#d4722e) gradient - represents the sunburst effect
  • Text: Cream (#ffe4b5) primary, warm beige (#c9a887) secondary - warm, readable tones
